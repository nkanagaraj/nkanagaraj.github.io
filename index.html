  • Fundraising

    The National Brain Tumor Foundation (NBTF) was founded by a group of people in San Francisco in 1981. It was an honor having to be a part of this great cause knowing that funds were being raised to help support these people and strengthen those around them. This graphic was created in Photoshop honoring a friend’s aunt who went through this battle.

  • Sketching

    Going to a creative art school allowed me to have an imaginative mind and it all started with drawing on a piece of paper. These sketches were created with the simple pencil and focuses on perspective and depth. The paintings are large replicas of wood, plaster and, granite - demonstrating that even with a paintbrush an illusion can be achieved.
